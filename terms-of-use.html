1. The App

No Panic is a self-help wellness app that offers calming exercises intended to help you manage moments of panic, anxiety, or stress. The App includes:

  • An Emergency panic button that opens a guided 10-step Panic Flow with short text tips and optional audio cues.
  • An Exercise hub with the following self-guided activities:
    • Breathing (Relaxing Breath, Square Breathing; configurable session length).
    • Grounding (5-4-3-2-1 sensory awareness).
    • Body Scan (10-zone progressive relaxation with adjustable pacing).
    • Visualization (10 themed guided visualizations with ambient audio).
    • Stretching (8 poses with configurable pacing).
    • Journaling (private, on-device notes with optional mood emoji).
    • Affirmations (daily card, favorites).
    • Meditation (planned; gated behind Pro where available).
  • An Onboarding walkthrough, a Settings screen, and an in-app Paywall for the optional Pro subscription.

The App is available in English and Ukrainian.
